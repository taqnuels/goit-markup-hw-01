Back Lat Pulldown

The Nautilus pulldown machine is a professional strength training device that is designed to effectively develop muscle strength in the upper body. This device is ideal for training the muscles of the back, shoulders and chest.
Thanks to the innovative pulldown system, the machine allows smooth and controlled movements, which is crucial for effective strength training. The adjustable load allows you to adjust the intensity of your workout to suit your individual needs,
making it suitable for both beginners and advanced athletes.
